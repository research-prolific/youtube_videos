What are you coding?

You will watch short videos posted on YouTube by real companies. Every video talks about or shows AI (artificial intelligence). Your job is to say what the video shows about AI empowering people or firms.

What is empowerment?

A video is empowering when it shows AI giving someone something new:

  • A new ability to do things (do more, do it faster, do it better)
  • A new way to create — art, writing, designs, music, ideas, personalized content
  • More control or choice in their work or their lives
  • New ways to act on their values — fairness, safety, ethics, inclusion, equity

The "someone" can be a person (a shopper, a worker, an artist, a citizen) or a firm (a company using AI).

How to watch each video

Watch the first 60-90 seconds of each video. Read the title and description shown below it. You do not need to watch the whole video.

Question 1: Is empowerment present in this video?

Yes — the video shows AI giving someone (a person or a firm) any of the four things listed above (new ability, new way to create, more control, or a new way to act on values).

No — the video is pure technology talk, business news, a corporate milestone (like an IPO or an award), or an AI mention without showing anyone benefiting.

Quick test: after watching, can you name a person or a firm who gains ability, creativity, control, or a values-aligned outcome? If yes -> Yes. If no -> No.

Question 2 (only if you answered Yes): Who is empowered?

Pick one. Ask yourself: who is DOING the action shown in the video?

Not who is on the screen. Not who is talking. Not who benefits at the end. Who is doing the action.

CategoryWhat it means
INDIVIDUAL A person — a shopper, a worker, an artist, a citizen. AI is a tool in their hands. They are doing the work; AI helps.
FIRM A company as a whole (and its AI). AI is the one doing the action; people watch or receive.

What if the video shows both?

Some videos mention both a firm action and a person action. Example: "Our AI handles the boring stuff so your team can focus on strategy."

In cases like this, code by the video's main point — the title, the first line of the description, or the first 30 seconds of talking:

  • If "your team can focus on strategy" is the main point -> INDIVIDUAL
  • If "AI handles the boring stuff" is the main point -> FIRM

Question 3 (only if you answered Yes): Which type of empowerment?

Pick one. Check the types in this order, and code the FIRST one that fits.

The order matters. This is the same order the AI classifier uses, so you and the AI apply the same rule.
  1. CREATIVE — Is the video mainly about AI making something new? Art, designs, writing, music, ideas, images, personalized content, campaigns. If yes -> code CREATIVE and stop.
  2. NORMATIVE — Is the video mainly about values? Ethics, fairness, safety, responsibility, sustainability, inclusion, equity, privacy, trust, responsible use. If yes -> code NORMATIVE and stop.
  3. AGENTIC — Is the video mainly about control, autonomy, or workforce identity? Someone deciding for themselves, overriding the AI, autonomous AI acting on someone's behalf, or a firm's people being upskilled / kept in the driver's seat / complemented by AI. If yes -> code AGENTIC and stop.
  4. OPERATIONAL — None of the above stood out? Code OPERATIONAL. This is the default: AI helps with a familiar task more efficiently. Save time, automate, streamline, do more with less.
